My Family

In my Indian culture, family is very close. I would consider my cousins (the two other kids in the middle picture) some of my closest friends. In my immediate family I have my brother who is a freshman in high school and my parents. They live in Alpharetta, GA which is about an hour from Athens. My parents had a very strong influence on my career choice. My mom works for Equifax, the credit-reporting company and my father works for SAP, a software-development company that makes SQL-based programs for businesses to use. Business played a big part in my childhood and I was constantly lectured and questioned about business situations in the news at that time.

Along with my immediate family, my cousins from my father's side are very close to me. They live in Boston but we see them every Christmas. The younger one is a junior in high school and the older one works at a consulting firm in downtown Boston. They both have helped me with countless situations and are very close to me. My older cousin has had the experience of going through the business track in college (she is a Pitt grad) and helped me narrow down the field of business I wanted to puruse. She knew of my affinity for tech and had a feeling I would strongly enjoy MIS. She introduced me to the basic concepts and I quickly knew this was the path for me.
